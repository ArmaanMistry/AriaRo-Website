What is AriaRo 2.0?

AriaRo 2.0 is a marquee 4-day symposium of USCI, Karnavati University that debuted in September 2022.

Now the core committee along with the faculty of USCI is planning to take it to the next level and make it bigger and better. The symposium will take place in 3 phases. Curtain raisers in March, exhibition 1 in July or August and main symposium in September.

The objective of this event is to bring students from different backgrounds and institutions on one platform to compete, learn and gain experience. Not only is it a knowledgeable experience but also a chance to connect to people and build on their social skills. The participants can apply online this website if interested.

The Theme of Ariaro 2.0 is:

  • Metaverse
  • Robotics
  • AR / VR
  • Cyber Security
